About Me

During my childhood I caught the music bug early and decided to pursue the itch later in college at the University of Redlands. After my graduation I spent a few years in Washington DC working for a non-profit, but soon after I returned to California.

Since then I've worked for a theater company, a indie record label and eventually wound up doing catalog management work for an online sheet music retailer for close to nine years. During my time there I rediscovered my interest in finding solutions to problems and with the help of the awesome dev team at the company, I decided to pursue an education in web development. In the spring of 2015 I decided to join the 17th cohort of General Assembly's Web Development Immersive and have since graduated in June.

If you're looking for assistance in the next steps of your project or are just starting out, please contact me at the information below. I look forward to working with you soon!
